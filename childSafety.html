eavesdrop CSAE policy and safety measures

last updated april 12, 2025

Commitment to Child Safety

eavesdrop is committed to creating a safe and enjoyable environment for users to share and discover music. We take the issue of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) seriously and have implemented measures to detect, prevent, and address any incidents of CSAE on our platform.

Age Restrictions and Verification

eavesdrop is intended for users aged 16 and above. Our age verification system ensures that users who are below this age are not permitted to use the platform. We use manual checks to verify that users adhere to this age requirement during sign-up.

3. Content Moderation and Reporting

We strive to maintain a platform free from harmful content. Our moderators actively review user profiles and ensure that all profile pictures comply with our safe-for-work (SFW) policy. However, we currently do not use automated content filtering tools. Reporting Process: Users can report any suspicious or inappropriate content (including CSAE-related material) through the app. Reports are handled by a dedicated internal team who will investigate and take appropriate action.

4. Data Handling and Privacy

User profile pictures are stored in a protected database, ensuring that they are secure. After any CSAE-related incident is resolved, user data is retained only for the legally required duration and is then permanently deleted.

5. Legal Compliance

eavesdrop complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We ensure that users under the age of 16 cannot access our platform. We also comply with other applicable legal requirements related to child safety.

6. Lack of External Partnerships

At this time, eavesdrop does not partner with external organizations such as NCMEC or Thorn, nor are we currently part of any industry group like the Tech Coalition or Safety by Design programs. However, we are committed to continually evaluating opportunities to collaborate with experts and improve our platform's safety measures.

7. Transparency and Policy Updates

While we do not currently publish periodic transparency reports, we regularly update our community on changes to our policies, including CSAE-related measures. These updates are shared through our app and website to keep users informed of our ongoing commitment to safety.

8. Moderator Training

Our moderators are trained to handle reports related to CSAE. This ensures that any incidents are managed promptly and appropriately, keeping the platform safe for all users.

9. User Education

We are working towards developing educational materials for users, to raise awareness about CSAE issues and the importance of a respectful and safe environment. These resources will be integrated into our app in the future.
